Welcome to Karma!

Karma encourages people to do better in their everyday lives by challenging them to record little positive actions, and then "nominate" their friends, passing it on.

Imagine like Snapchat, but instead of selfies and filters, it's self-care and environmental conservation (trust me, this is so much cooler than it sounds...)

Karma works with a network of your friends to nominate and strive to do better than - let's add your first friend on Karma!
